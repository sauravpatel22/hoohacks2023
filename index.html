Our goal is to make people knowledgeable about ways that they can have a more sustainable household. We understand that different people have different living situations, so not everyone can do everything, but we want to demonstrate that everybody can do something. Small changes that are wide-spread across a community can have a big impact and we want to show that everyone can make an impact that fits their lifestyle.

Using your responses from our survey, we will generate a list of suggestions that will help decrease your energy usage, saving our planet and your wallet! Every suggestion is personalized for your house and your energy usage.
